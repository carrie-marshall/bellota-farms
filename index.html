Welcome to Bellota Farms — How to Use This App
① Breeding Tracker (this tab)
Start here. Click + Add Female to register each sow with her breeding attempt dates and boar. Once confirmed pregnant, click Mark Confirmed Bred to lock in her expected farrow date and assign a farrowing hut.
② Litter Records
When a sow farrows, click + New Litter to create her record. Enter piglets as they are born on the Birth tab. Record weights, vaccines, and health events in subsequent tabs. She auto-appears in the Breeding Tracker.
③ Herd & Locations
Track where every animal is at any given time. Active litters appear here as a sow+piglet unit. After weaning, individual pigs appear in Growing Pigs. Use Group Move to move multiple animals at once.
④ Task List
Auto-generated from your data — vaccines, weight checks, weaning dates, estrus checks, and more. Tasks are read-only; check them off as you complete them. They disappear when the underlying record is updated.
⑤ Season Summary
A production overview by year. Shows total pigs alive, designation breakdown, and the master piglet roster. Use the dropdown to switch between seasons. Designations set here update the pig's record everywhere.
⑥ Estimator
A planning scratchpad — completely separate from your real records. Add estimated litters, allocate pigs to product types, and see projected availability windows. Use this to answer customer inquiries or plan seasons in advance.
All data is saved automatically to your browser. Use the Export button on each litter card to download records as CSV or Excel.
